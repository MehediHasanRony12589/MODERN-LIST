TIG-400A Welding Machine Specifications

Model: TIG-400A
Product Description: A high-performance portable TIG welder suitable for various welding applications.

Features

  • Advanced MOSFET technology, energy-efficient and low noise.
  • Shielding gas post-flow function, stable and mild welding arc.
  • HF start circuit ensures high success rate in arc striking.
  • Automatic over-voltage, over-current, and over-heat protection.
  • Novel design, compact size, portable yet durable.
  • Applicable for welding various kinds of metals, such as carbon steel, stainless steel, alloy steel, copper, and other non-ferrous metals.
  • Complete with TIG torch, electrode holder, earth clamp, face mask, and hammer.
