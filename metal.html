Well-come TO Metal Shop

What is Metal Shop?

The metal shop is equipped with equipment for welding, cutting, bending, and grinding steel, aluminum, and other metals. While most processes are performed by individual users, precision machining and welding can be accommodated by speaking with Lab staff and technical assistants. As with other areas of the Lab, all materials must be supplied by the individual user.
