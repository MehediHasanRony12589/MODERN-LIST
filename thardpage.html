FLEXIBLE PRODUCTION LINE TRAINING SYSTEM

Equipment Working Principle

Workpiece in stock is supplied in sequence. Once a workpiece is detected, a pneumatic swing cylinder moves the material block to the handling station. At the handling station, actuators move the material to be processed and sorted based on sensor data.

Main Technology Parameters

  • Power Supply: AC220V ± 10%, 50Hz
  • Robot Model: ANNO ROBOT 602 Equivalent to IRB120/3KG/0.58
  • Repeat Positioning Accuracy: 0.01mm
  • Operating Range: +165° to -165° for rotation axis
  • Speed: 250°/s for various axes
  • Temperature: -10°C to 40°C; Humidity ≤90%
  • Machine Capacity: ≤5KVA

Learning Scope

  • Sensor Adjustment Training
  • Air Circuit Connection Training
  • PLC Program Training
  • Handling and Assembly Station Control Training
  • Industrial Robot Handling Station Control

Specifications

  • Distribution Station Dimension: 460 x 800 x 1270 mm
  • Handling Station Dimension: 460 x 800 x 1050 mm
  • Assemble Station Dimension: 460 x 800 x 1160 mm
  • Industrial Robot Station Dimension: 900 x 900 x 1110 mm
