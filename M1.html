Feature

The machine is designed with multi-function of drilling, broaching, reaming, tapping, and facing milling. With a strengthened capacity of drilling, it allows the workpieces to be drilled with a larger range of size. It is suitable for use in both production and maintenance shops:

  • Easy operation.
  • Casting iron structure for long durability.
  • Column type of vertical drilling machine.
  • Worktable can rotate 180 degrees and tilt 45 degrees.
  • Worktable lifting by hand.
