AL-602A Pneumatic Training Device

Description

The AL-602A system, in addition to regular pneumatic experiments and pneumatic technology curriculum design, is a typical experimental equipment that combines mechanical and electrical systems perfectly.

It is designed to meet the requirements of modern pneumatic professional teaching experiments, following teaching materials such as "Hydraulic and Pneumatic Transmission" and "Pneumatic Control Technology."

System Features

  • Meets the practical application needs of the teaching system
  • Firm structure with good stability
  • Low noise operation
  • Clear and intriguing layout design

Special Features

The four truckles with crack slots allow for easy movement and installation on the work table. Panel grooves are spaced at 25 mm intervals for easy component placement.

Various pneumatic components are in separate modules with elastic pins, making them easy to insert into the general aluminum panel, allowing for flexible layouts and clear pneumatic circuits.

The electrical control units include an independent relay control unit and a multi-button control unit, providing a simple and clear control interface.

High safety features include over-current protection. The power supply cuts off if earth leakage exceeds 30mA, and the system uses a DC 24V power supply with over-current protection, preventing damage due to improper operation. The system's nominal pressure is 0.6MPa.

Pneumatic Basic Control Circuit Experiments

  • Single acting cylinder automatic control
  • Double acting cylinder automatic control
  • PLC control technical application
  • Relay control technical application
  • Comprehensive and expandable pneumatic circuits
